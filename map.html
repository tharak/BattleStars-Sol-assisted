Successor Stars — strategic map Universe → System → Body → Battle

Real (to-scale, log-compressed for legibility) positions and sizes: bodies sit at their true current orbital angle and distance. Click a body to zoom in, click a breadcrumb (or Zoom Out / Esc) to zoom back out. Three factions (Blue, Green, Red) each start with a 12-ship fleet near their home planet, visible on the System map; click a fleet to select it, then click a destination to move it, or click the same fleet again to open Formation Setup. Move two different factions' fleets close enough together and a Battle button appears, handing off to the existing battle rules prototype.
