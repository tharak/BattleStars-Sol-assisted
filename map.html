Successor Stars — strategic map Universe → System → Battle

Real (to-scale, log-compressed for legibility) positions and sizes: bodies sit at their true current orbital angle and distance. The System map is a real 3D (isometric-style, no perspective distortion) view of the Star map and every planet's moons in one -- drag to rotate, right-drag or arrow keys to pan, scroll (zooms toward the cursor) or -/= to zoom, click a planet to zoom in on its moons (invisible at a glance, same as in real life) or the Sun to reset the view. Click a breadcrumb (or Zoom Out / Esc) to leave a screen. Nothing on the map carries a floating label -- click a body or a ship and its name shows in the panel at the top right instead. Three factions (Blue, Green, Red) each start with a 12-ship fleet near their home planet; click a fleet to select it, then click a destination to move it, or click the same fleet again to deselect -- the panel also shows its formation while selected, with buttons to change it on the spot. Move two different factions' fleets close enough together and a Battle button appears, dropping straight into a real tactical battle between those two fleets, right here (their actual formations, no navigating away) -- spectated, since the map doesn't have a single "you" yet. The same rules engine also runs standalone as a battle rules test bench, for trying scenarios/fleet sizes/control modes this view doesn't expose.
