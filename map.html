Successor Stars — strategic map Universe → System → Battle

Real (to-scale, log-compressed for legibility) positions and sizes: bodies sit at their true current orbital angle and distance. The System map is a real 3D (isometric-style, no perspective distortion) view of the Star map and every planet's moons in one -- drag to rotate, right-drag or arrow keys to pan, scroll (zooms toward the cursor) or -/= to zoom manually -- moons are invisible at a glance, same as in real life, so zoom in by hand to see them. Click a breadcrumb (or Zoom Out / Esc) to leave a screen. Nothing on the map carries a floating label -- click a body or a ship and its name shows in the panel at the top right instead. Three factions (Blue, Green, Red) each start with a 12-ship fleet near their home planet, and every ship is individually selectable and commandable with the same controls the tactical battle screen uses: Q/E turn, W forward, S back (costs all remaining MP), F arms fire mode (or just click a highlighted enemy in range/arc/LOS to fire directly), Space ends the activation. Set Course repositions the selected ship anywhere on the map in one instant jump, for real interplanetary distances the hex-by-hex MP budget can't cover. Selecting a flagship also reveals its command area and can move every friendly ship inside it as one formation. The same rules engine also runs standalone as a battle rules test bench, for trying scenarios/fleet sizes/control modes this view doesn't expose.
